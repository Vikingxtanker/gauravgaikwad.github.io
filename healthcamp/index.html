About Pharmacy Practice Lab

The Pharmacy Practice Lab (PPL) at Dr. D.Y. Patil College of Pharmacy, Akurdi, was inaugurated on 29th April 2025. It is a dedicated and modern facility specifically designed for Pharm.D. students, aimed at bridging academic learning with practical clinical exposure.

The lab features 10 fully equipped computers and a lecture room with a smart board, creating an interactive learning environment. It supports key services such as Health Screening Tests, Adverse Drug Reaction (ADR) Reporting, and Drug Information Centers (DIC), providing students with practical experience in real-world pharmacy operations.

Students also train using clinical tools like Micromedex for drug interactions, evidence-based decision-making, and patient counseling.
