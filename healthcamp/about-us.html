About the Pharm.D Program

Pharm.D is a comprehensive program designed to prepare students for clinical roles in healthcare settings, combining pharmaceutical sciences with practical hospital training. The course spans six years, including five years of classroom learning and one year of hands-on internship or residency in clinical or hospital environments.

There is also a 3-year Pharm.D (Post Baccalaureate) option for B.Pharm graduates, which includes two years of academics and one year of internship.

Pharm.D holders have excellent prospects in hospitals, pharmaceutical industries, clinical research, regulatory affairs, academia, and healthcare consulting. They can work as clinical pharmacists, drug safety associates, medical writers, regulatory affairs managers, or start entrepreneurial ventures.

The course prepares students for an emerging and vital role in healthcare focusing on precise medication management and clinical pharmacy services, offering diverse career paths with growing demand in India and globally.

This makes Pharm.D an excellent choice for students interested in pharmaceutical healthcare and clinical patient care.
