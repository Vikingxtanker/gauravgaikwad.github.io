Mudgal Shoulder Mobility & Strengthening

Exercise Overview

  • Stand tall with feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Hold the mudgal handle firmly, allowing it to hang naturally.
  • Swing forward to shoulder height, then backward in a controlled motion.
  • Rotate the shoulder inward and outward while keeping your torso stable.
  • Perform 6–10 swings per arm for 2–3 sets.
  • Breathe steadily: inhale on preparation, exhale on the swing.

Benefits

  • Improves shoulder mobility and joint lubrication.
  • Strengthens the rotator cuff and stabilizer muscles.
  • Enhances balance, proprioception, and coordination.
  • Rooted in ancient Indian physical training traditions.
